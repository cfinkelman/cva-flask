METHODOLOGY, LIMITATIONS, & REGULATORY DISCLOSURES

Genomic positions for which analysis was requested were amplified from genomic DNA by PCR. Resultant amplified DNA underwent dideoxy sequencing with fluorescent terminators. This report describes the results of sequence analysis performed only at the genomic loci requested by the ordering party. Other regions of the gene and genome were not analyzed. Genomic coordinates refer to GRChr37.

This test was developed and its performance characteristics determined by the Rady Children's Institute for Genomic Medicine - Clinical Genome Center. It has not been cleared or approved by the U.S. Food and Drug Administration. The FDA has determined that such clearance or approval is not necessary. This test is used for clinical purposes. Clinical correlation and genetic counseling are recommended. This laboratory is certified under the Clinical Laboratory Improvement Amendments as qualified to perform high complexity clinical laboratory testing.
